Revision of Article.

Session Laws 1967, c. 1060, s. 1, rewrote the former Article, which also consisted of 11 sections and was derived from Session Laws 1945, c. 723, s. 1; c. 1087; 1949, c. 430, ss. 1, 2; c. 1292; 1951, c. 1048, ss. 1, 2; 1957, c. 541, s. 19. Where sections were similar to prior provisions, the historical citations were added thereto.

Editor’s Note.

Former G.S 165-1 pertained to North Carolina Veterans Commission renamed. Former G.S 165-2 pertained to references changed. Former G.S 165-3 pertained to definitions. Former G.S 165-4 pertained to purpose.

Session Laws 2015-241, s. 1.1, provides: “This act shall be known as ‘The Current Operations and Capital Improvements Appropriations Act of 2015.’ ”

Session Laws 2015-241, s. 33.6, is a severability clause.

Chapter 134. Youth Development.

§§ 134-1 through 134-39: Recodified as G.S. 134A-1 to 134A-39.

Editor's Note. - This Chapter was rewritten by Session Laws 1975, c. 742, effective July 1, 1975, and recodified as Chapter 134A. Chapter 134A was subsequently repealed by Session Laws 1998-202, s. 1(a); see notes under G.S. 134A-1 through 134A-39.

Chapter 71. Indians.

§§ 71-1 through 71-20: Repealed by Session Laws 1977, c. 849, s. 1.

Cross References. - For present statute partially covering the subject matter of repealed G.S. 71-1 to 71-12, see G.S. 71A-1 to 71A-6.

For present provisions as to the North Carolina State Commission of Indian Affairs, see G.S. 143B-404 to 143B-411.

State Government Reorganization. - The Commission on Indian Affairs was transferred to the Department of Administration by a Type II transfer by Session Laws 1977, c. 849, s. 1, effective July 1, 1977.

Chapter 153B. Mountain Resources Planning Act.

Sec.

  • 153B-1 through 153B-4. [Repealed.]

§§ 153B-1 through 153B-4: Repealed by Session Laws 2013-413, s. 43. For effective date, see editor's note.

History

(§ 153B-1: 2009-485, s. 1; repealed by 2013-413, s. 43. § 153B-2: 2009-485, s. 1; 2010-142, s. 14; repealed by 2013-413, s. 43. § 153B-3: 2009-485, s. 1; 2010-142, s. 15; 2010-180, s. 7(g); 2011-372, s. 1; repealed by 2013-413, s. 43. § 153B-4: 2009-485, s. 1; 2010-142, s. 16; repealed by 2013-413, s. 43. For effective date of these repeals, see editor's note.)

Editor's Note. - Former G.S. 153B-1 pertained to title, legislative findings and purpose. Former G.S. 153B-2 pertained to definitions. Former G.S. 153B-3 pertained to the Mountain Resources Commission. Former G.S. 153B-4 pertained to the Mountain Area Resources Technical Advisory Council.

Session Laws 2013-413, s. 61(b), provides: "Except as otherwise provided, this act is effective when it becomes law [August 23, 2013]." Session Laws 2013-413, s. 60(c), had provided that: "This act becomes effective July 1, 2015." Session Laws 2014-115, s. 17, amended Session Laws 2013-413, s. 60(c), to replace the reference to "This act" with the words "This Part."

Session Laws 2013-413, s. 61(a), is a severability clause.
